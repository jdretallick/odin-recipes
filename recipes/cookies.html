Cookies

These are my secret family recipe chocolate chip cookies. My caretaker would bake these a lot for me and my siblings growing up. I learned the recipe and would then mainly eat the dough.

Ingredients

  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of sugar
  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of brown sugar
  • (250 g) 1 cups of room temp butter
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of chocolate chips
  • (500 g) 2 cups of flour
