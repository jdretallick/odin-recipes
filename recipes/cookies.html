Cookies

These are my secret family recipe chocolate chip cookies. My caretaker would bake these a lot for me and my siblings growing up. I learned the recipe and would then mainly just eat the dough. It was tastier and less work to eat the raw dough.

Ingredients

  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of sugar
  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of brown sugar
  • (250 g) 1 cups of room temp butter
  • 1 egg (optional)
  • (125 g) 0.5 cups of chocolate chips
  • (500 g) 2 cups of flour

Steps

  1. Add the sugar to the butter, mix until creamed.
  2. (Optional) add egg and mix.
  3. Mix in flour slowly.
  4. Mix in the chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop ball of the dough onto a pan. The balls should be the same diameter as a spoon you'd use to eat soup.
  6. Bake at 350 F for 12 mins.

Makes about 24 cookies. Be sure to share them or you will get type 2 diabetes.
